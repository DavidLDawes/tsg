Traveller Sector Generator

Generate and Save Sectors

Click the link above to generate randomized star systems and sector maps for the Traveller Science Fiction Role-Playing Game.

Source code for this page and the Sector Generator JS is here.

The generator creates detailed planetary systems with characteristics including:

  • Star types and configurations (single, binary, triple)
  • Planetary systems with various world types
  • Universal World Profiles (UWP)
  • Starports, atmospheres, populations, governments
  • Trade classifications
This is based on the Traveller SRD core rules, specifically the World Creation bit. I took liberties to add star and planetry system details,

Traveller Small Craft Design

Design Small Craft

Click the above link to design Traveller SRD compatible small craft (non-jump vehicles) with at most 100 tons and at least 10 tons. These are non-capital ships. To design regular starships below 3,000 tons click on the Design Ships button in the Traveller Non-Capital Ship Design section that follows next. For capital ships of 3,000 tons or more use the subsequent Design Capital ships block.

Source code for the Small Craft Designer is here,

This is based on the Traveller SRD core rules, specifically the Spacecraft Design bit for Capital Starships of 3,000+ tons.
